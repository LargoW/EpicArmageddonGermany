Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Daemonic Beasts removed, replaced with Beasts of Nurgle and Plague Drones.
  • Death Guard Terminators formation slightly cheaper due to the list's lack of Dreadclaws.
  • Plague Zombies are fixed size, but slightly worse stats.
  • Plague Tower oriented more towards being a transport option, with higher speed and worse save.
  • Nurgle Titans trades Void Shields for more DC, retains the same speed as others of their type.
