Version 1.0.beta

The EA:Ragnarök Snakebite Clan Ork list is the EA:Ragnarök version of a Feral Ork list. We felt the existing Feral Orks lists were a bit one-sided and frustrating to play against, with an overload of large 4+ reinforced armor War Engines. We have done major changes compared to the Feral Ork list, while trying to keep the army theme intact and your existing models relevant.

The Ork infantry has been changed for the Snakebite list into primitive versions of their more advanced brothers, with less advanced weaponry. The new transport has been introduced, the Gargantuan Squiggoth which is in between the Squiggoth and the Orkeosaurus in size. The Orkeosaurus has received a significant reduction to its armor, but is now faster. A maximum of two Steam Gargans can be taken per formation, and the critical hit effect has been changed, but you can now have multiple formations of Steam Gargants in your army. A few new units has been introduced to give the list access to some shooting, like the Lobbas and the Squiggoth Supa-Lobba.
