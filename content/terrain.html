Optional Terrain Rules

Some types of terrain are much more common than others, due to the unbalancing influence that certain types of terrain can have on the game. For this reason, here are a set of optional/expanded general terrain rules and concepts (Certain types of player-placed terrain such as Bunkers, Trenches etc. are covered by the Fortified Positions special rule.))
